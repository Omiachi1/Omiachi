Below are the eligible candidates to apply for the scheme

Undergraduate Student

Graduate Student

A graduate student is someone who has earned a bachelor's degree and is pursuing additional education in a specific field.

Masters Student

These includes a student pursuing a Master's degree.

Activities

Applications are available for the Norwegian Presidential Scholarships 2024 in Norway. Students from all countries can apply for Fully Funded Scholarships in Norway to pursue Undergraduate Degree Programs and master’s degree Programs. No Application Fee will be charged to students. All the Academic Subjects like Business, Management, Law, and economics are available

The prestigious Bi Presidential Scholarship for Norway offers possibilities to international students each year. The master’s degree program lasts two years, whereas the bachelor’s degree program lasts three years. Registration for fully financed Norway scholarships is open to both international and Norwegian students. The subjects and topics offered by the Norwegian government’s scholarships for developing nations are diverse.
